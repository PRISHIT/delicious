    “Your ration of you and for you”

    We want to reach everyone by providing them their right to healthy foods. Your Ration is formed with a goal to provide quality food to everyone. We are dealing in wheat flour, Rice, Sugar,Pulses, Mustard oil, and Spices. After ensuring the qualitative parameters we purchase the raw material directly from the farmers so that we can provide our customers with pure and natural products which in turn will be healthy for them. We don’t mix any chemical or any other things in our product to make the same attractive.

    Wheat Flour (Gehu ka atta)

    Wheat flour is generally being used by every household but they face the issue of getting the good quality wheat. When they go to the chakki for milling of the wheat or when they directly go for the packaged wheat flour then in some of the cases they face the issue of Mixture e.g. Maida is mixed. We are here as a solution by providing them good quality natural wheat flour which is produced by using 100% good quality wheat.

    Rice

    We sell the Rice in a packed form, we are dealing in various varieties of Rice. We don’t mix any fragrance or even don’t alter the size of the rice. We are here to provide you the same thing as we get from the farms. If you are getting the fragrance then that is completely natural not of any spray or any other thing.

    Sugar

    Sugar is essential for every household. Even in the case of sugar, we are facing the issue with the taste. We are here to solve this issue, We purchase the sugar from the chini mill, collate the same size of sugar and then pack them in a pack of 1 KG,2 KG, and 5 KG.

    Pulses(Toor Dal)

    There are so many types of pulses but are only dealing in Toor dal most commonly known as Arahar ki dal because the maximum number of households use toor dal. We don’t use any polishing technique or any other thing we use the unpolished toor dal and pack it in a pack of 1KG and 2 KG to reach our customers.

    Mustard Oil (Sarso ka tail)

    Quality of oil plays a vital role in the health of anyone. We use the best quality black sarso which we produce on our farms. We use only mustard to produce oil and ensure that we can provide the best quality of oil to our Customers.
